Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change my plan anytime?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate any billing differences.

Is there a free trial for Premium?

Yes, we offer a 7-day free trial for our Premium plan. No credit card required to start your trial.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and for corporate plans, we can arrange invoicing and bank transfers.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Absolutely. You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. Your access will continue until the end of your current billing period.
